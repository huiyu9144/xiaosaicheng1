❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How to switch language?

A: Go to Settings, click on Language to switch between Chinese and English.

Q: How to change timezone?

A: Go to Settings, click on Timezone to select your desired timezone.

Q: What is the data source for matches?

A: We integrate multiple public data sources to provide you with comprehensive match information.

Q: How often is the data updated?

A: Match data is updated in real-time based on data sources to ensure you have the latest schedules.
